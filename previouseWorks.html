From Case Challengs to Case Studies

3DE Case Challenge

A partnership between the 3DE program and a business to solve problems in a team setting, using the combination of primary and secondary data. Such problems are solved using the 3DE competencies: creativity and innovation, cultural agility, self-direction, effective collaboration, engaging communication, and critical and analytical thinking.

During my time in 3DE, I have participated in many case challenges and case studies that have helped me grow both as a person and a student. On a personal level, it has let me grow into a confident public speaker and team leader. As a student, I have been able to develop both soft skills and hard skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, collaboration skills, and digital literacy in-office application such as GSuite and Office 365

This self-growth due to case challenges are not without results, as I have paced as a finalist and winner in some case challenges. The height of my career in 3DE can be seen in the photo above, where I can be seen posing with my team and judges for winning my Home Depot Presentation, along with my winning presentation that can be seen in the gallery below the picture.
